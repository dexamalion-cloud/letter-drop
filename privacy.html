Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

About Droplet

Droplet is a free word game. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you play.

Information We Collect

Droplet stores your high scores locally on your device using your browser's local storage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Advertising

Droplet uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to show you personalised ads based on your visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings.

Cookies

We do not set any cookies ourselves. Our advertising partner Google AdSense may set cookies for the purpose of serving advertisements. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information.

Third Party Links

Droplet does not contain links to third party websites other than those required for advertising.

Children's Privacy

Droplet is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy please raise an issue on our GitHub repository.
