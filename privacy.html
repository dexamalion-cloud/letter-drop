Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026  ·  playdroplet.com
Daily Droplet is a free word puzzle game. We don't require you to create an account, and we don't collect your name or email address. Here's a plain-English summary of how data works on this site.

Who we are

Daily Droplet is operated as a personal project and is available at playdroplet.com. If you have questions about this policy, you can reach us at privacy@playdroplet.com.

What data we collect

When you play Daily Droplet, we store the following information in Google Firebase (our database provider):

  • An anonymous player ID — a randomly generated identifier stored in your browser. This is not linked to your name, email, or any personal information.
  • Your game results — your score, number of words, and the date you played. This is used to power the leaderboard.
  • Your streak — how many consecutive days you've played, stored against your anonymous ID.

We do not collect your name, email address, location, or any other personally identifiable information.

Cookies and local storage

Daily Droplet uses your browser's local storage (not cookies) to remember:

  • Your anonymous player ID, so your streak and history persist across visits
  • Whether you've already played today's puzzle
  • Your sound preferences

This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to any third party other than Firebase for leaderboard purposes.

Advertising

Daily Droplet displays advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to show you ads based on your interests. You can learn more about how Google uses data at policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting google.com/settings/ads.

Third-party services

We use the following third-party services to operate the game:

  • Google Firebase / Firestore — stores anonymous game results and leaderboard data. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google AdSense — serves advertisements. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts — loads fonts used in the game interface. Google Privacy Policy
  • GitHub Pages — hosts the game files. GitHub Privacy Statement

Data retention

Anonymous game records are retained indefinitely to power historical leaderboards. Since records are not linked to any personal information, there is nothing to delete that would identify you.

You can clear your local data at any time by clearing your browser's local storage for playdroplet.com (available in your browser's developer tools or privacy settings).

Children's privacy

Daily Droplet is a general-audience game suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The game requires no registration and collects no personal data.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision. Continued use of the game after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at privacy@playdroplet.com.
